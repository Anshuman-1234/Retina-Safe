What We Detect

Four conditions. One platform.

Retinal diseases progress silently — most are only detected after irreversible damage. RetinaSafe screens for the four most common retinal and ocular conditions using AI analysis and functional vision testing.

💧

Diabetic Retinopathy

Damage to retinal blood vessels caused by diabetes. Detectable through microaneurysms, haemorrhages, and exudates — and through contrast sensitivity loss measured by our Contrast Discrimination Game.

Contrast Game CNN Analysis
🎯

Macular Degeneration

Degeneration of the macula — the central retina responsible for fine detail vision. Our Dynamic Amsler Grid detects metamorphopsia (wavy vision) validated against clinical-grade paper tests.

Amsler Grid Drusen Detection
👁️

Glaucoma

Progressive optic nerve damage that destroys peripheral vision in arcuate patterns. Our Peripheral Reaction Tester maps 8 visual field zones with 32 calibrated flashes — modelled on Humphrey perimetry.

Perimetry Game Field Mapping
🌈

Cataract

Yellowing and opacification of the crystalline lens causes characteristic blue-yellow colour discrimination loss. Our Hue Sorting Game isolates tritan axis deficiency with 10 timed rounds across increasing difficulty.

Hue Sorting Tritan Axis

How It Works

From image to insight in minutes

A seamless four-step process combining structural retinal imaging with functional vision assessment — generating a clinically structured risk report.

1

Capture Fundus Image

Upload a retinal fundus photograph captured via a fundus camera, smartphone adapter, or clinic equipment. Our preprocessing pipeline handles normalization and quality validation.

📷 Camera / Adapter ~1 min
2

AI Retinal Analysis

Our CNN model analyses the fundus image for microaneurysms, haemorrhages, exudates, optic disc abnormalities, and vessel irregularities — generating probability-based risk indicators per disease class.

🧠 CNN Model ~30 sec
3

Vision Game Assessment

Four scientifically designed games assess functional retinal performance: contrast sensitivity, central distortion, peripheral field, and colour discrimination. Each score adjusts AI model confidence.

🎮 4 Games ~6 min
4

Risk Report & Referral

A structured risk report with Low / Moderate / High classification, annotated findings, recommended next steps, and nearby ophthalmologist listings. Downloadable for your doctor.

📋 PDF Report Instant

Gamified Vision Testing

Science disguised as play

Each mini-game is grounded in validated psychophysical testing protocols. Gamification improves engagement, encourages repeat screening, and enables longitudinal data collection over time.

01
🌫️

Contrast Discrimination

Detects Diabetic Retinopathy

Identify a subtly different object against a grey background across 10 difficulty levels — from 19.6% contrast down to 1.6%. Modelled on the Pelli-Robson Contrast Sensitivity Chart. Minimum 30 trials for statistical reliability.

10 levels 30+ trials ~3 min
02
⊞

Dynamic Amsler Grid

Detects Macular Degeneration

Monocular grid test: mark any lines that appear wavy, missing, or distorted. Canvas uses weighted zones — central foveal markings carry 4× the weight of peripheral. Validated against paper Amsler with 0.9+ correlation.

2 eyes tested 4 weight zones ~2 min
03
✦

Peripheral Reaction Tester

Detects Glaucoma

Fixate on the centre while peripheral dots flash for 200ms across 8 visual field zones — NW, N, NE, E, SE, S, SW, W. 4 flashes per zone. Consistent misses in a zone indicate probable scotoma. Modelled on Humphrey perimetry.

8 zones 32 flashes ~1 min
04
🎨

Color Hue Sorting

Detects Cataract

Find the tile with a subtly different hue — shifting along the blue-yellow tritan axis that cataract selectively impairs. 10 timed rounds, starting at +18° hue shift down to +1.5°. Modelled on the Farnsworth D-15 Dichotomous Test.

10 rounds 30s limit ~30s

Why It Matters

Preventable blindness is a solvable problem

Vision impairment due to retinal diseases is one of the leading causes of preventable blindness worldwide. Most retinal disorders progress silently — detected only after irreversible damage has occurred.

RetinaSafe acts as a first-line screening system, enabling early intervention especially in underserved regions where specialist access is limited, equipment costs are prohibitive, and awareness is low.
