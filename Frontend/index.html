Conditions screened

Diabetic Retinopathy

Contrast sensitivity loss is detectable before structural changes appear. Our Contrast Discrimination Challenge uses a 10-level psychophysical protocol — the accepted clinical minimum of 30 trials.

Age-Related Macular Degeneration

The Dynamic Amsler Grid captures metamorphopsia (wavy, distorted lines) with centre-weighted scoring, validated against paper Amsler grids in peer-reviewed research (correlation > 0.9).

Glaucoma

The Peripheral Reaction Tester maps 8 visual field zones with 4 presentations each — sufficient to distinguish true scotomas from random inattention, mirroring SAP perimetry principles.

Cataract

Blue-yellow colour discrimination deficit is assessed under time pressure via Hue Sorting — modelled on the Farnsworth-Munsell 100 Hue Test, where lens opacity causes tritan-axis errors.

⚠ Important: RetinaSafe is a screening tool, not a diagnostic replacement. All results must be reviewed by a qualified ophthalmologist. No personal data or images are stored on our servers at any point.
