🩺 Disease Library

Conditions We Detect

Our AI models are trained to identify these retinal conditions with high accuracy

🔴

Age-Related Macular Degeneration

AMD affects the macula, the central part of the retina. It causes blurred or reduced central vision and is a leading cause of vision loss in older adults.

🔵

Cataract

A cataract is a clouding of the eye's natural lens. It can cause blurry vision, faded colors, and difficulty seeing at night. Usually develops slowly over time.

🟣

Diabetic Retinopathy

Caused by damage to blood vessels in the retina due to diabetes. It can lead to blindness if untreated. Early detection through screening is critical.

🔴

Glaucoma

A group of conditions that damage the optic nerve, often due to high intraocular pressure. Known as the "silent thief of sight" because it progresses without symptoms.

🟡

Hypertensive Retinopathy

Damage to retinal blood vessels caused by high blood pressure. Symptoms include narrowing of vessels, retinal hemorrhages, and cotton wool spots.

🟢

Normal (Healthy)

A healthy retina with no detectable abnormalities. Regular eye exams are still recommended to maintain eye health and catch any changes early.
