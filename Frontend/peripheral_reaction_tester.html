How to Take This Test

  • A blue cross will appear and drift gently at the center of the screen. Keep your eyes focused on it throughout the test.
  • Small blue dots will briefly flash at the edges of your screen.
  • Press the Spacebar, tap the screen, or click as soon as you notice a dot.
  • The test consists of 10 trials and takes about 40–70 seconds.
  • Try not to move your eyes to search for dots — rely on your peripheral vision.
