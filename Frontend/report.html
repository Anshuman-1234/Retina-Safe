Disclaimer

RetinaSafe provides risk stratification scores based on retinal image analysis and vision-based functional assessments. It is not a substitute for professional medical examination or diagnosis by a qualified ophthalmologist.

Screen luminance is not calibrated — contrast scores are relative indicators, not absolute clinical thresholds. The Amsler grid requires monocular testing; incomplete eye coverage may suppress monocular scotomas. The peripheral test requires maintained central fixation. Monitor colour gamut affects hue sorting accuracy.

All data is processed locally in your browser. No personal data, images, or results are transmitted or stored by RetinaSafe.
