Step 1 of 3 — Vision Games

Before your retinal scan,
let's assess your vision.

You will play 4 short games (2–3 minutes each) that measure functional aspects of your vision our AI cannot detect from an image alone. Each game is clinically grounded and contributes directly to your risk report.

1
Contrast Discrimination Challenge ~3 min · Diabetic Retinopathy signal
2
Dynamic Amsler Grid ~2 min · AMD / Macular signal
3
Peripheral Reaction Tester ~2 min · Glaucoma signal
4
Colour Hue Sorting ~1 min · Cataract signal

Sit in a well-lit room. Use this device at a comfortable reading distance.

Game 1 of 4  ·  Contrast Discrimination Challenge
Level: 1/10 Attempts: 3 remaining Consecutive fails: 0/3

Find the shape

A shape is hidden on the background. Click or tap it before the timer runs out.
The contrast decreases with each level — making it harder to distinguish the shape.

10 levels · 3 attempts per level · Game ends on 3 consecutive failures

Game 2 of 4  ·  Dynamic Amsler Grid
Eye: Right Eye Phase 1 of 2

Mark what looks distorted

Stare at the central dot on the grid. Without moving your eyes, paint over any areas that look wavy, blurry, dark, or missing.

You will do this once per eye. Cover the other eye with your hand.

👁 Cover your LEFT eye. Test your RIGHT eye first.

Keep your gaze on the central dot. Paint distorted areas.

Game 3 of 4  ·  Peripheral Reaction Tester
Flash: 0/32 Detected: 0 Missed: 0

Track the centre. React to the edges.

Keep your cursor (or finger) on the moving circle at the centre of the screen at all times. Randomly, small dots will flash at the edges of your vision.

Press Space (or tap the screen) the moment you notice a peripheral dot. Do not move your eyes to look for the dots — let them come to you.

32 flashes across 8 zones · ~64 seconds total

Tap anywhere to respond to a dot
Game 4 of 4  ·  Colour Hue Sorting
Round: 1/10 Score: 0 Time: 30s

Find the different tile

You will see a grid of coloured tiles. All tiles are the same colour except for one "imposter" that has drifted slightly on the blue-yellow hue axis.

Click the imposter tile as quickly as you can. The difference shrinks with each round. You have 30 seconds for all 10 rounds.

10 rounds · 30 seconds total · Hue difference reduces each round

Find and click the different tile

✓

All 4 games complete.

Your functional vision scores have been recorded. The next step is to submit your retinal image for AI analysis. Both sets of data will be combined to generate your risk report.
